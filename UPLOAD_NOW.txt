GITHUB UPLOAD - 3 STEPS
=======================

STEP 1: Open PowerShell in this folder (client-github)

STEP 2: Run this command (replace YOUR_USERNAME):
  .\upload.bat YOUR_USERNAME

  Example:
  .\upload.bat john-doe

STEP 3: Wait for completion

Done! Your repo will be at:
  https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/speedcopy-client

---

If upload.bat doesn't work, use QUICK_UPLOAD.txt for manual steps.
